Non-Medical Companion Support for Seniors

Calm, reliable companionship and safety presence for older adults in Fort Smith, Springdale, Fayetteville, and surrounding communities.

Scheduling note: I schedule by area to keep visits consistent and avoid rushing between homes.

What I Do

  • Friendly company & conversation
  • Reading, television, games, and companionship
  • Light meal support & kitchen cleanup
  • Medication reminders only (client self-administers)
  • Local errands & grocery pickup
  • Safety supervision & reassurance
  • Routine support: reminders, calming wind-down, setting out water/snacks

Important Boundaries

Strictly non-medical, non-intimate companion care.
I do not assist with bathing, toileting, dressing, lifting/transfers, wound care, injections, skilled nursing tasks, or medical decision-making.

This service is for presence, supervision, conversation, and light household support so families can rest. If personal care is needed, I’m happy to coordinate around a family caregiver or professional aide.

In an emergency, I call 911 and notify the family contact.
